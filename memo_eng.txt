In addition to the above, various other elements are under development to be added!
*The UI is being manipulated to add various elements and change the display.

・Additional Buildings
Transportation：Low-cost version of railroad (for small countries)
Hospital：Reduce state pollution effects and state mortality rates

・Additional Company 
Medical Corporation：Hospital Employees Standard of Living and academics Investment Pool contribution

・Additional Diplomatic Actions
Additional bankroll, Further bankroll, Burned-out bankroll, Last bankroll：More bankroll
Full Exemption of Payment Obligations：Complete exemption of dependent countries from payments
Policing support：Decreased state penalties due to social unrest in dependent states, political campaign activity, rate of revolutionary progress, and rate of secession progress
Food support：Loans to dependent countries for food security and Pop.

・Additional Journal
Establish a company in an additional dependent country of the journal (start with a decision)
Forced immigration of people to less populated areas (start with a decision)

・More information in the subjects section of the Diplomacy tab.
